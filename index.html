Short biography

César Ernesto Hernández Hernández was born in Empalme, Sonora, Mexico, in 1985. He received a bachelor’s degree in Electronic Engineer from the Hermosillo Institute of Technology (“ITH Instituto Tecnológico de Hermosillo” in Spanish), in Hermosillo, Sonora, Mexico in 2008. He received an Master degree (M.Sc. in Physics and Mathematics) from the University of Granada and the University of Castilla-La Mancha, Spain, in Spain 2010. Furthermore, he received an Master degree in Industrial Computing and a Ph.D. degree in Informatics from the University of Almeria (UAL), Spain, in 2013 and 2017, respectively. In addition, he has also got a Master in Systems and Control Engineering at the Complutense University of Madrid and the National University of Distance Education (UNED) in 2022.

From August 2010 to December 2011, he worked as Adjunct Professor at the Department of Industrial Engineering at the Sonora Institute of Technology (“ITSON Instituto Tecnológico de Sonora“ in Spanish), in Empalme, Sonora, Mexico. In May 2012, he joined as a Predoctoral Fellow the Automatics, Robotics and Mechatronics Research Group, University of Almeria, Spain, where he did his doctoral thesis and did teaching work. During his doctoral thesis, he conducted research stays at the University of Seville, Spain, with Professors Carlos Bordons Alba and Miguel Angel Ridao Carlini, and at the Federal University of Santa Catarina, Brazil, with Professor Julio Elias Normey Rico. He was working as a researcher at the University of Almeria until October 2017.

Once he got the Ph.D. degree, in August 2018 he joined the Technological Automotive Centre of Galicia (CTAG) as Data Scientist, where he was involved in projects related to the automotive sector and Industry 4.0 and research projects. From February 2019 to December 2020 he worked at Basque Center for Applied Mathematics (BCAM) as Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning researcher as a Postdoctoral Fellow, where he was working on topics related to energies and image processing. From January 2021 to December 2021 he worked at Deusto Institute of Technology, Engineering Faculty, the University of Deusto as a Research Associate in applied Control Systems, Machine Learning and, Artificial Intelligence, where he worked in topics related to horticulture and energies. He was working for a short period of 3 months (march to may, 2022) as Senior Data Scientist at We Are Clickers SL, where he performed BigData processing and analysis tasks using pySpark. He is currently working at ESIGELEC | Écoles d’ingénieur-es généralistes as Research Engineer in applied control, where he is developing mathematical modelling and automatic control strategies (PID, MPC, Fuzzy) applied to aviation systems.

He is currently a member of the Spanish Committee of Automatic (CEA).

His research interest includes Microgrids/Smartgrids, Mathematical Modelling, Model Predictive Control, PID Control, Intelligent Control, Control Systems, Renewable Energies, Wind Turbines, Engineering, Optimization, Machine Learning and Deep Learning, Big Data, and Data Mining.
